About FYJC Portal

The FYJC Portal is a dedicated platform designed to support Standard 11 Science students at Elphinstone College. It aims to provide easy access to study materials, video lectures, and practice resources in an organized and professional manner.

Through this portal, students can explore various subjects including Physics, Chemistry, and Biology, with resources tailored for academic preparation, exam readiness, and practical understanding.

Our goal is to make learning interactive, accessible, and efficient for every student, helping them achieve excellence in their studies and build a strong foundation for future academic success.
